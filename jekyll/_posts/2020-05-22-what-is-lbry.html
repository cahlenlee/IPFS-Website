The benefits of LBRY are that it is open-source and decentralized, which means it is safe to use and censorship resistant. If you already have content on YouTube, you can set it up to automatically duplicate it on the LBRY network, and it will continually add your new videos on YouTube to your LBRY channel as you upload them.
