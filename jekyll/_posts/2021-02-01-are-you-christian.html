The Bible contains some amount of truth, but that does not mean it's the only source or even the best source of truth. Also, the translated Bible uses the word "God" to mean several different things, so the untranslated usage is going to be important for understanding each context.

As for Jesus, there's no way to know for sure that he ever really existed. While there are many things he reportedly said and did which I do not resonate with, there are others which I do. So you see, it's not so simple of a question to answer.
