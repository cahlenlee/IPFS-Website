We all have preferential states of being. There is a difference between getting punched in the face and getting a high five.

Natural Law determines the results of the actions we choose, and it effectively serves back to us what we dish out. If we want to live in a world where our harm from others is minimized, then we must choose to act in a way which minimizes our harm to others.
