The front and back containers sitting on the racks can hold roughly a month's worth of food, reducing the number of times I need to risk going into populated areas during a crisis. The electric motor runs almost silently, making it easy to get around without being noticed.

I plan to do a video showing you how to build this step-by-step sometime in the future. In the meantime, let me know if you have any questions.
