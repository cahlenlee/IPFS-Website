This time with the new Yurt I hope to ward off the mold using a homemade Wood Polish made of 1 Part Beeswax, 2 Parts Caprylic Acid and some Lavender Essential Oil. I decided to add Borax at the end to decrease flammability and to add a third anti-mold ingredient.
