For the sake of simplicity and personal economics I usually have white rice and vegetables for dinner, but every once awhile when I drive through a big town I decide to treat myself to a real hearty meal. I use a 12-Volt Slow Cooker powered by my single 100W Solar Panel and 100ah Battery. It also runs great plugged directly into the truck's cigarette lighter while on a long drive.

It's always nice to arrive home to a healthy meal that's hot and ready to eat, eh?
